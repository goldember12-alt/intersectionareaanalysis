## AADT-Normalized Aggregate Rate Exhibit Specification

Exhibits EX13 and EX14 use only stakeholder-safe aggregate outputs from `work/output/roadway_graph/analysis/current/descriptive_crash_rate_suppression_review/`.

Required fields:

- `analysis_window` or `signal_relative_direction`
- `assigned_crash_count`
- `estimated_vehicle_mile_exposure`
- `crashes_per_million_vmt`
- exact 95% CI: `exact_rate_lower_95_per_million_vmt` and `exact_rate_upper_95_per_million_vmt`
- caveat flags: bidirectional AADT assumption, `DIRECTION_FACTOR` not applied, missing/review AADT excluded, unit-level rates remain QA-only

The exhibit is a descriptive AADT-normalized prototype. It is aggregate only and makes no causal, policy, comparative-performance, or downstream functional area distance interpretation.

## Context Feature Relationship Exhibit Specification

Exhibits EX15 through EX21 use accepted 0-2,500 ft context cross-tabs and denominator-ready aggregate rate cells. Count heatmaps are descriptive assigned-crash counts. Rate tables are aggregate prototype cells only and show denominator warning, sparse cell, and review notes rather than hiding high cells. The former rate display summary is technical QA only.

Rate display rules:

- assigned crash count >= 20
- estimated vehicle-mile exposure >= 5,000,000
- denominator-ready unit count >= 25
- stable AADT coverage share >= 0.80
- bidirectional AADT assumption retained
- `DIRECTION_FACTOR` not applied
- missing/review AADT excluded from denominator values
- unit-level rates remain QA-only

Estimated exposure is calculated from AADT, represented roadway length, and the 2022-2024 crash period. Direction factors are not applied in this prototype. These exhibits are pre-regression descriptive context relationship summaries and do not make causal, policy, comparative-performance, or downstream functional area distance interpretations.
